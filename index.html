The Color Blue

The color blue is often associated with calmness, responsible, sadness in a piece of art.
The color blue is often seen as non-threatening and is a sign of stability and reliability.
It can be seen in Pablo Picasso, Wassily Kadinsky, Yves Klein are just some artist
that focus on the color in their piece. For more info on Pablo Picasso, Wassily Kandinsky,
and Yves Klein. The color blue is considered to be one of the most common favorite color.
